# Crew Roster & Job Rotation Reference

## Crew Setup

2 crews, each with:
- 1 shovel
- 1 water bucket (5-gallon)
- 1 cylinder (bottomless 5-gallon bucket, inverted)
- 1 can of cooking spray (cylinder lubricant)

## Cylinder Prep

Spray the inside of the cylinder with cooking spray before each pour to prevent sand from sticking to the plastic. Reapply as needed.

## Job Roles (Rotating)

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|---------------|
| Water Hauler | Fetches water and pours it into the cylinder |
| Sand Loader | Shovels sand into the cylinder |
| Stomper | Compacts the water/sand mixture inside the cylinder by stomping |
| Brusher | Uses paintbrushes to remove loose sand from the formed block |
| Carver | Sculpts and carves detail from the compacted sand block |

Roles rotate frequently — everyone should cycle through each position.

## Schedule

- **Tuesday** — Practice (figure out roles, test cylinder prep, dial in the sand/water mix)
- **Wednesday** — Build day

## Block Count

28 bucket-blocks per story (see [`structures/castle-layout.svg`](structures/castle-layout.svg) for placement).
